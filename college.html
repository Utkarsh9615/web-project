Ch. Braham Prakash Government Engineering College

Chaudhary Braham Prakash Government Engineering College (CBPGEC) is a premier government engineering institute located in delhi,india.It was establised by the Department of Training and technical education.Government of National Territory of Delhi in 2007 and was named after the first Chief Minister of Delhi,Chaudhary Braham Prakash.The aim of the government to develop the excellence in the field of civil and environment enginering.The college is affilated to Guru Gobind Singh Indrapastha University,a state university developed by Govt of NCT of Delhi.
